Privacy policy

App privacy

Tock does not collect, store, or share personal data. The app has no accounts, analytics, or tracking.

  • Data collected: None
  • Tracking: None
  • Accounts: Not required
  • Third-party services: None

If you contact support, you may choose to share information (for example, logs) in order to help troubleshoot an issue. Any information you provide is used only to respond to your request.

Website analytics

This website uses Google Analytics 4 to understand general usage such as page views and traffic sources. Google Analytics is configured with IP anonymization enabled by default. This analytics applies only to the website and is not used by the Tock app.
